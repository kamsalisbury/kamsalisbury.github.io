Solo-entrepreneur and Small Businesses may elect to use free email options for production business use, regardless Terms of Service. It is important to understand iCloud's capabilities and limitations before investing time in using the platform for business operations.

1. The iCloud storage paid tier (50GB $0.99/month USD) from a technical perspective, supports the same file storage and sharing services as the free tier (5GB) [https://support.apple.com/en-us/HT201318](https://support.apple.com/en-us/HT201318) service
2. iCloud storage file restore queues previously deleted files for 30 days [https://support.apple.com/kb/PH21250?locale=en_US&amp;amp;viewlocale=en_US](https://support.apple.com/kb/PH21250?locale=en_US&amp;amp;viewlocale=en_US)
3. iCloud storage file restore operations are only accessible from the iCloud.com web interface [https://support.apple.com/en-us/HT201104](https://support.apple.com/en-us/HT201104)
4. A 2015 macOS 10.11 bug (and older) created a situation where unsynchronized files moved by the user were deleted on iCloud. (Yet another reason to update and upgrade your macOS!) [http://txfx.net/2015/07/14/apple-icloud-drive-deletes-your-files/](http://txfx.net/2015/07/14/apple-icloud-drive-deletes-your-files/)
5. Any type of file 50GB and less in size can be saved to iCloud storage [https://support.apple.com/en-us/HT201104](https://support.apple.com/en-us/HT201104)
6. iOS devices with a current Apple ID sign in and the iCloud backup setting enabled, will backup to iCloud only when connected to charging power and screen locked
7. iCloud on macOS and Windows, with a current Apple ID sign in and iCloud configured will be able to access iCloud stored data, but not iOS backups directly
8. iCloud on macOS and Windows, is not a backup storage destination for macOS or Windows OS backups [https://support.apple.com/en-us/HT201060](https://support.apple.com/en-us/HT201060)
9. iCloud Family Sharing supports up to six accounts. However, iCloud Family Sharing does not allow access to each others data (Similar to Office365, Box and others), even though the storage and apps purchased by the parent is shared [https://support.apple.com/en-us/HT202305](https://support.apple.com/en-us/HT202305)
10. iCloud Email attachments are limited to 20MB (5GB when using Airdrop) [https://support.apple.com/kb/PH2629?locale=en_US](https://support.apple.com/kb/PH2629?locale=en_US)
11. Maximum recipients per an email = 500
12. Maximum recipients per an email per a day = 1000
13. Maximum emails per a day = 1000
14. It is not possible to configure iCloud Email to send email as you@yourdomain.tld (Through clever email forwarding from you@yourdomain.tld much of the same service is achievable, however your emails will still be sent from you@icloud.com
15. Reporting and adjusting SPAM settings is not obvious [https://www.engadget.com/2013/08/21/how-to-avoid-icloud-email-spam/](https://www.engadget.com/2013/08/21/how-to-avoid-icloud-email-spam/) or listed for review, anywhere [http://www.macworld.com/article/2029570/silent-email-filtering-makes-icloud-an-unreliable-option.html](http://www.macworld.com/article/2029570/silent-email-filtering-makes-icloud-an-unreliable-option.html)
16. No direct control of SPAM settings (you never even see a log of rejected emails) [https://support.apple.com/en-us/HT202315](https://support.apple.com/en-us/HT202315)
17. [iCloud API access exists](https://developer.apple.com/icloud/)
